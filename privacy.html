Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  IberoSync Ltd, United Kingdom

IberoSync ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect information when you use our website or services.

1. Who we are

IberoSync is a digital marketing agency based in the United Kingdom. You can contact us at hello@iberosync.com with any privacy-related questions.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact information you provide via our onboarding form (name, email, phone, business address)
  • Business information (business name, sector, social media handles, website)
  • Payment information processed securely via Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • Communications you send to us by email or other means
  • Basic analytics data when you visit our website (pages visited, browser type)

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide and manage the services you have contracted with us
  • Send you service-related communications and reports
  • Process payments for our services
  • Improve our website and service offering
  • Comply with our legal obligations

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR: performance of a contract (to deliver our services), legitimate interests (to improve our services and communicate with you), and your consent where explicitly provided.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted third-party service providers necessary to deliver our services, including:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Google (Drive, Gmail, Looker Studio)
  • Notion (client portal management)
  • Tally (form collection)
  • Metricool (social media scheduling)

All third parties are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

6. Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you are a client and for up to 6 years afterwards for legal and accounting purposes. You may request deletion at any time subject to our legal obligations.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your personal data. You also have the right to data portability and to object to processing based on legitimate interests. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@iberosync.com.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic analytics. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at hello@iberosync.com.
